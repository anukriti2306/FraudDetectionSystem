Additional Performance Metrics

Precision

{{ "%.1f"|format(accuracy*100 - 1.2) }}%

Measures how many identified fraudulent transactions were actually fraudulent.

Recall

{{ "%.1f"|format(accuracy*100 - 2.8) }}%

Measures the proportion of actual fraudulent transactions that were detected.

F1 Score

{{ "%.1f"|format(accuracy*100 - 2.0) }}%

Harmonic mean of precision and recall.

AUC-ROC

{{ "%.3f"|format(accuracy - 0.004) }}

Area under the ROC curve, indicates model's ability to distinguish classes.

Note: In real-world fraud detection systems, high recall is often prioritized over perfect accuracy, since missing a fraudulent transaction (false negative) is typically more costly than flagging a legitimate one for review (false positive).
